About Exato

29 years of EHS expertise at your company's service

Consultoria Exato combines technical rigor, strategic vision, and deep knowledge of Brazilian legislation to deliver real solutions in occupational safety, environmental management, and radiation protection.

  • Production Engineer from UFSCar, with specializations in Environmental Management (UNICAMP) and Occupational Safety Engineering
  • Experience at major multinationals in the pulp and paper sector — Votorantim, International Paper, Sylvamo, CETESB
  • Former CETESB consultant and participation in Watershed Committees (Rio Grande, Mogi Guaçu)
  • Specialist in safety culture transformation and international EHS audits
  • Licensed by ANSN as Radiation Protection Supervisor for industrial nuclear gauges
